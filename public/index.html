Trusted Pest Control Service For Sacramento Homes & Businesses

1. Are your pest control treatments safe for families and pets? +

We use carefully selected pest control products and treatment methods designed to effectively manage pest activity while helping maintain a safe environment for families and pets when applied properly.

2. How often should pest control services be scheduled? +

Most Sacramento properties benefit from routine pest control service every month, bi-monthly, or quarterly depending on pest pressure, property conditions, and the type of service plan.

3. What should I do before a pest control appointment? +

Clear access to affected areas, remove clutter where pests are active, secure pets, and let the technician know about any recent pest sightings or problem areas.

4. Do you provide follow up pest control services? +

Yes. Follow up service can be included when needed to help monitor pest activity, reinforce treatments, and support long term prevention.

5. Can pests return after treatment? +

Yes, pests can return if entry points, moisture issues, food sources, or outdoor activity remain. Ongoing prevention helps reduce the chance of repeat infestations.
